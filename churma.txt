Ingridients:
-wheat flour
-desi ghee
-sugar/khand

Description:
1. Prepare Roti(as per quantity needed 3 for 1 person) with wheat flour.
2. Start crushing rotis with your hands and adding ghee and sugar to it.(ensure that roti are hot and ghee is in liquid form)
3. Stop adding ghee when the crushed rotis start stickin and add sugar as per taste.
4. Stop crushing when all three are properly mixed and soft to eat.

Churma is ready. Enjoy!!
